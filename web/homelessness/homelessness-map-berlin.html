Overview

This page collects map-ready coordinates for organizations listed in the homelessness directory. The embedded Google map gives a Berlin map context; the numbered markers and table below are maintained by the Knowledge Hub from cited organization or public directory sources.

The map loads Google Maps. Visitors who do not want to load Google services can use the coordinate table and source links instead.

Density layer status

A colored density layer is planned, but it should not be drawn from guesswork. To avoid misrepresenting where homeless people are, the hub needs a source-reviewed dataset or a carefully governed field observation process before showing hotspot colors.
