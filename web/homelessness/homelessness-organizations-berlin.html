Overview

This page combines the Berlin organization list with a map-oriented coordinate directory. It is a starting point, not a complete service map. Always check the linked organization pages for current opening times, phone numbers, donation needs, and volunteer requirements. The entries below are drawn from the organizations' own pages and public Berlin-facing service descriptions.12379

The embedded map loads Google Maps. Visitors who do not want to load Google services can use the coordinate table and source links instead.

Density layer status

A colored density layer is planned, but it should not be drawn from guesswork. To avoid misrepresenting where homeless people are, the hub needs a source-reviewed dataset or a carefully governed field observation process before showing hotspot colors.
