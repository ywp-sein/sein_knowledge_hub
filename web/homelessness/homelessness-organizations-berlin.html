Overview

This page combines the Berlin organization list with a map-oriented support directory. It is a starting point, not a complete service map. Always check the linked organization pages for current opening times, phone numbers, donation needs, and volunteer requirements. The entries below are drawn from the organizations' own pages and public Berlin-facing service descriptions.12379

The embedded map loads Google Maps. Visitors who do not want to load Google services can use the organization table and source links instead.

Citywide mobile outreach		 Winter outreach Responds to calls, visits known sleeping places, distributes warm items, and can organize transport to emergency accommodation when wanted. DRK Berlin: Wärmebus

Rütlistraße 18, 13407 Berlin		 Addiction counselling, low-threshold social advice, therapy placement, street work Teen Challenge Germany describes counselling, street work, housing/life-help and support for people on the margins; Berlin Hilfelotse lists Teen Challenge Berlin with low-threshold offers for people with drug addiction and general social counselling. Density layer status

A colored density layer is planned, but it should not be drawn from guesswork. To avoid misrepresenting where homeless people are, the hub needs a source-reviewed dataset or a carefully governed field observation process before showing hotspot colors.
