Overview

This list is a starting point, not a complete service map. Always check the linked organization pages for current opening times, phone numbers, donation needs, and volunteer requirements. The entries below are drawn from the organizations' own pages and public Berlin-facing service descriptions.123

Organizations and services

Organization Focus How it helps Link
Berliner Stadtmission Cold aid, emergency overnight support, clothing, outreach, medical and social support Runs Kältehilfe offers including Kältebus and emergency overnight places. Armut und Wohnungslosigkeit
DRK Berlin Wärmebus Winter outreach Responds to calls, visits known sleeping places, distributes warm items, and can organize transport to emergency accommodation when wanted. DRK Wärmebus
Berliner Obdachlosenhilfe e.V. Volunteer street tours and social support Runs aid tours with meals, drinks, clothing, sleeping bags, hygiene items, conversation, and information. Berliner Obdachlosenhilfe
Caritas Berlin Housing emergency counselling and medical outreach Offers housing emergency advice and runs medical first/basic care such as the Caritas Arztmobil. Menschen in Wohnungsnot
Gangway e.V. Street social work Works with homeless and housing-threatened adults through Drop Out and StreetBer teams in public spaces. Team Drop Out
Unionhilfswerk Housing emergency assistance and accommodation Provides projects for people who are homeless or at risk of homelessness, including Wendepunkt Wohnen and shelters. Wohnungslosenhilfe
