Purpose

The SEiN Knowledge Hub is a shared place for turning scattered information into understandable knowledge. It is meant to help people ask better questions, compare sources, understand systems, and find practical next steps with care and integrity.

The hub separates general research methods from topic-specific articles. Method pages explain how to study a social issue. Issue pages gather what is known about a concrete subject and connect that knowledge to real support routes.

Current pages

  • How to research a social issue Method page for defining questions, checking sources, mapping systems, and keeping revision notes.
  • Homelessness in Berlin First issue page with urgent help, official support routes, and links to the topic subpages.
  • Knowledge Hub version log Development record for every new update to the website and its content.

Future expansion

The wiki will expand into more issue categories over time. Each new page should keep the same basic habits: clear scope, traceable sources, practical pathways, and a visible revision timestamp whenever content changes.
