Third-party material

External sources, linked pages, names, logos, and quoted material remain under their own terms. Check the original source before reusing third-party material.

The Knowledge Hub should normally summarize and link to external sources instead of copying their text. Short quotations should be used only where necessary, clearly attributed, and kept close to the claim they support. Organization names are used for identification; logos, photographs, maps, datasets, and downloadable documents should be reused only when the original source permits that use.

Content reliability

The website is an information hub, not legal, medical, social-work, or emergency advice. Pages about urgent support, eligibility, opening hours, addresses, phone numbers, or legal rights should cite current source pages and be checked before a reader relies on them.
